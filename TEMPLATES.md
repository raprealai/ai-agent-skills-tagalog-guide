# Agent Skills Templates

> Ready-to-use templates para sa iyong sariling agent skills. Copy, edit, and deploy!

---

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Choose a template** that fits your need
2. **Copy the entire content**
3. **Create a new `.md` file** (e.g., `my-skill-name.md`)
4. **Fill in the bracketed sections** `[like this]` with your own content
5. **Test it** with your AI assistant
6. **Iterate** based on results

Replace lahat ng `[bracketed text]` with your specific info

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Be Specific** - The more detailed your skill, the better the output
2. **Use Examples** - Real examples > generic ones
3. **Define Tone Clearly** - Specify formality, language mix, emoji use
4. **Include Edge Cases** - What to do when [unusual situation]
5. **Update Regularly** - Refine based on what works
